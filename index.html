Our Love Story

❋

A beautiful journey of two hearts finding their way to each other

Video Call that changed it all.....

31st August

Two screens. Two hearts. One spark. What began as a simple video call turned into a story whispered by the stars. We didn’t need candlelight or violins — just the shimmer in our eyes as we spoke for the very first time. Miles apart, yet something clicked — like our souls recognized each other from lifetimes ago. That night, two hearts began to hum the same melody.

It wasn’t just a call… it was the beginning of everything.

Match got Fixed

4th September

When families nodded, the universe clapped. The stars were aligned, and so were our names — woven into horoscopes, echoed in blessings. What started as a spark turned into a sacred bond, as both our families said:

“Yes, they are meant for each other.” The world may call it "Fix Aindi", but to us, it felt like destiny whispering, "You're finally home."

Marriage Discussions

13th September

Words met hearts, and laughter became tradition. Families met, rituals began, and smiles danced across every face. But our eyes? They kept stealing glances in between every chant and every sweet served. With every “maata”, our bond deepened, and with every “muchata”, the butterflies fluttered louder.

“It wasn’t just a gathering, it was our love being celebrated in verses and vibes.”

First Meet after Match got Fix

14th September

The first look after the yes — shy, sweet, and spark-filled. No screen between us. Just hearts, awkward giggles, and lingering eye contact. We met like rain meeting parched soil — gentle, overwhelming, beautiful. The air was shy, time was short, but oh — the moment was infinite.

“That first meet was a poem written in silence… and we were the only ones who could read it❤️”

Engagement

5th October

Rings exchanged. Promises made. Smiles locked forever. Amongst twinkling lights, a thousand winks from stars, and a gathering of those we hold close — we sealed our bond. The ring wasn’t just a circle of gold. It was a vow. A beginning. A forever. Hands trembling, eyes glistening, hearts full.

“From this day on, my hand fits perfectly in yours — in every lifetime.”

Pelli (Wedding Day)

30th October

Sacred fire. Sacred vows. Sacred love. Under the golden canopy, we circled the divine flame — each step a promise, each mantra a heartbeat, each glance a universe unfolding. The air was thick with jasmine, blessings, and emotion. And when I looked into your eyes,

"It felt like the universe paused to watch us become one." “Our souls danced around the fire… and never let go.”

Reception

1st November

Our love dressed up for the world. Lights, laughter, and the loud thump of celebration. We walked in — not as two, but as one — with hearts full and hands forever held. Every camera flash caught a piece of our fairytale. But the real sparkle?

It was in how we looked at each other… like we’d finally come home. “If the wedding was sacred, this night was magic.”
